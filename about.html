Our Story

SpoRecords has been a cornerstone of Dubai's music scene for over 20 years. We're dedicated to preserving the art of vinyl and sharing our passion with fellow music lovers.

How It All Began

SpoRecords was founded in 2000 by two classmates, Afsah and Aathrey, who shared an undying love for vinyl records. What began as a small shop in Karama, Dubai has grown into one of the city's most respected record stores.

Our journey started with a humble collection of second-hand records. Today, we offer thousands of titles spanning every genre from rare collector's items to the latest releases.

Over the years, we've survived the digital revolution and vinyl's resurgence, always staying true to our commitment to quality music and exceptional customer service.
